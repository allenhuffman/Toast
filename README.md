# Toast
Vaughn Cato's 3-D vector maze engine for CoCo 3 OS-9.

# History
During the early 1990s, I was messaging with Vaughn Cato and somehow (maybe he remembers; I don't) the discussion about whether or not a 3-D maze engine (like "Doom") could be done on the Radio Shack Color Computer.

He went to work and created this "toast" demo program. Why is it called toast? (Maybe he remembers; I don't.)

Looking at the timestamps of the files from my old CoCo OS-9 disk, this version is from October and November 1994. (See screenshot.)
